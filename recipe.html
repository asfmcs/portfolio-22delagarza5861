Guilt Free Protein Pancakes

Credits
Ingredients
1 1/2 Scoop Protein Pancake mix
2 Egg Whites
1/2 Mashed Ripe Bananna
1 tsp Vanilla Extract
2 tsp Baking Powder
1/4 cup Cashew Milk

Directions:

  1. In a medium bowl, whisk together egg whites, banana, almond milk, baking powder, and vanilla extract.
  2. Mix in Quest Protein Powder and cinnamon.
  3. Spray a nonstick pan with cooking spray or coconut oil and heat over low-medium.
  4. Scoop 3-4 tablespoons of batter per pancake onto pan.
  5. Cook pancakes 2 to 3 minutes each side, or until golden brown.
  6. Add in banana slices, choco chips, blueberries anything you want before flippin

Spinach Hummus

Credits
Ingredients
1 can of drained and rinsed chickpeas
Large handful of spinach
2 tbsp of tahini
1 juice of lemon
2 tbsp of olive oil
1 small garlic clove
1/4 Cup of water
1 tbs of cilantro

Directions:

  1. Blend all ingredients together in a food processor.
  2. For extra spice, add smoked paprika and cayenne pepper.

Keto Almond Butter Cookie Skillet

Credits
Ingredients
1 cup almond butter
2 Eggs
1/3 cup monk fruit (or sweetener of choice- if replaced with stevia I could recommend 1/8 cup bc it's so sweet)
1 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp vanilla bean paste (vanilla extract)
1/2 cup of sugar free dark chocolate chips

Directions:

  1. Mix in all ingredients together, at the end add in the cocholate chips.
  2. Coat a small pan with coconut oil and pop them in the oven for about 12 mins! Keep an eye out for them I like my cookies crispy on the outside and gooey on the inside, so I recomend taking it out at 10 mins!
  3. This time I made them in a small brownie pan but I have also done individual cookies which work great and in a skillet topped of with coconut ice cream... amazing.
